# Nano Syllabus Pricing

> Source: https://nanosyllabus.com | Last updated: 2026-06-01

Nano Syllabus offers a free plan for everyday study, with a paid plan for
students preparing for competitive entrance exams (IOE, CEE, KU, Loksewa).

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Who it's for | Included features |
|------|-------|--------------|-------------------|
| Free | Free (always) | All students, Classes 6–12 | Chapter-wise doubt solving in English and Nepali · Teacher-reviewed notes · Smart summaries · Auto-generated flashcards · Basic progress tracking |
| Entrance Pro | Contact for pricing | Students preparing for IOE, CEE, KU, Loksewa | Everything in Free · 10 years of solved past papers · Adaptive mock tests · Weakness analytics · Topper strategies · AI study planner tied to your exam date |
